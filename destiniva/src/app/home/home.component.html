Destiniva Tokens (DST)

DST is a EIP20 Compliant token used as a saving mechanism token on the Destiniva.io booking platform. You are able to use your DST to purchase flight tickets, reserve hotel stays, and even rent vehicles, all for a much lower price than with traditional fiat.

In other words, these serve as reward points on the platform. But inlike traditional reward point system, DST tokens have real value and allow you to have full control over them (Descentralized). You can use them to save money when booking your getaway, send to family and friends, and more.
